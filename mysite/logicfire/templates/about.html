About LogicFire

Your gateway to the world of manga

Our Story

Founded in 2023, LogicFire was created by manga enthusiasts for manga enthusiasts. We noticed a lack of high-quality, ad-free manga platforms with a focus on user experience, so we decided to build our own.

Our Mission

To provide manga fans with a seamless reading experience, high-quality translations, and a community where they can discover new titles and connect with fellow fans.

Features

  • Thousands of manga titles
  • Regular updates
  • Personalized recommendations
  • Reading lists and bookmarks
  • Community discussions
